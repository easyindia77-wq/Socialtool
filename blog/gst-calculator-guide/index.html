What is GST?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is an indirect tax applied on the supply of goods and services. It has replaced multiple taxes like VAT, service tax, and excise duty in many countries including India.

GST is divided into different slabs such as 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28%, depending on the type of product or service.

Types of GST Calculation

There are two main types of GST calculations:

  • Add GST: When GST is added to the base price
  • Remove GST: When GST is already included in the total amount

GST Formula Explained

For adding GST:

GST Amount = (Original Price × GST Rate) / 100

Total Price = Original Price + GST Amount

For removing GST:

GST Amount = Total Price − (Total Price / (1 + GST Rate/100))

Original Price = Total Price − GST Amount
