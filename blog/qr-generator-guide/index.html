What is a QR Code?

A QR code (Quick Response code) is a type of barcode that stores information in a machine-readable format. It can contain URLs, text, phone numbers, email addresses, and other data.

QR codes are designed to be scanned quickly using a mobile camera, making them extremely useful for modern digital communication.
